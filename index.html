The Knight's Tour & Warnsdorf's Algorithm

The Knight's Tour is when a knight visits each square of a chess board once and only once. The most efficient algorithm for performing the Knight's Tour is with Warnsdorf's Algorithm. I made a change to existing instances of outputting the result of the algorithm by mapping the order of moves to the alphanumeric notation that is standard for chess players to know.
